# TorqueBudget-Privacy-Policy
Privacy Policy for TorqueBudget Android App

Effective Date: 5/25/2026 TorqueBudget ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

**Data Collection & Use**

**Notification Data:** We access your notifications solely to identify transaction alerts from specific banking apps. This data is processed locally and used to populate your transaction history.

**Storage:** All financial records are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your bank accounts or personal data.

**Advertising:** We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and use data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
